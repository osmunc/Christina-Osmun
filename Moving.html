    • Why the Move?

    What I am Looking for in an Employer

    Having experience across the world has provided me with a unique insight into what I want from a company and which companies will most benefit from me as an employee. My goal is to form a long term relationship with the next company I join. As a result, I will be selective about choosing companies that allow me to use my best skills and that can help me to developed them overtime in way that will be mutually beneficial.
